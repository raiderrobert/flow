# Aesthetic Directions

When selecting an aesthetic direction, pick one and commit fully. Mixing multiple directions without intent produces incoherent design.

## Dark & Moody

### Brutalist Luxury
- **Feel**: Raw power meets refined taste
- **Typography**: Serif display (Playfair Display, Bodoni Moda) + monospace labels (IBM Plex Mono)
- **Color**: Near-black backgrounds, gold/champagne accents, cream text
- **Spacing**: Generous — let the darkness breathe
- **Details**: Sharp corners (0px radius), uppercase labels with wide letter-spacing, hairline borders
- **Shadows**: None — use border (#1F1F1F on #0F0F0F) for depth

### Neon Terminal
- **Feel**: Cyberpunk command center
- **Typography**: Monospace display (JetBrains Mono, Fira Code) + clean sans body
- **Color**: Black (#000), single neon accent (green #00FF41, or cyan #00D4FF), low-opacity grid overlays
- **Spacing**: Dense, data-rich — grid-based
- **Details**: CRT scanlines, blinking cursors, terminal borders, text-shadow glow
- **Shadows**: Colored glow (`0 0 20px rgba(0,255,65,0.3)`)

### Noir Editorial
- **Feel**: Late-night magazine, film noir
- **Typography**: High-contrast serif (Cormorant, Newsreader) + thin sans (Outfit light)
- **Color**: Deep charcoal (#1A1A1A), warm off-white text (#F5F0E8), single warm accent
- **Spacing**: Very generous — editorial whitespace
- **Details**: Thin rules, pull quotes, dramatic scale contrast
- **Shadows**: Subtle warm shadows

## Light & Refined

### Swiss Minimal
- **Feel**: Rational clarity, Swiss design tradition
- **Typography**: Neutral grotesque (Satoshi, Albert Sans) — hierarchy through weight and size only
- **Color**: Pure white, stroke-based borders (#E4E4E7), single primary blue
- **Spacing**: Strict 8px grid, visible alignment
- **Details**: No shadows, 1px borders, circular status indicators, visible grid structure
- **Shadows**: None — borders and background shifts only

### Warm Organic
- **Feel**: Scandinavian hygge, wellness
- **Typography**: Rounded geometric (Outfit, Nunito Sans) — friendly, approachable
- **Color**: Warm cream (#F5F4F1), forest green (#3D8A5A), terracotta (#D89575)
- **Spacing**: Generous, airy — lots of whitespace
- **Details**: Soft shadows, generous radius (12-16px), rounded everything, warm palette
- **Shadows**: Soft, warm-tinted

### Paper Editorial
- **Feel**: Print magazine brought to screen
- **Typography**: Serif headings (DM Serif Display, Fraunces) + sans body (General Sans)
- **Color**: Off-white/cream paper (#FAF8F5), near-black ink (#1A1A1A), single accent
- **Spacing**: Column-based, magazine layout with gutters
- **Details**: Drop caps, pull quotes, thin rules, bylines, image captions
- **Shadows**: None — paper-flat

## Bold & Expressive

### Pop Art
- **Feel**: Warhol meets digital
- **Typography**: Black-weight grotesque (Cabinet Grotesk, Clash Display) — impactful
- **Color**: Bold primaries (red, blue, yellow) on black or white, high saturation
- **Spacing**: Tight — density creates energy
- **Details**: Hard edges, thick borders, halftone patterns, rotated elements
- **Shadows**: Hard drop shadows (no blur, offset only)

### Geometric Bauhaus
- **Feel**: Constructivist, architectural
- **Typography**: Geometric sans (Sora, Urbanist, DM Sans) — precision
- **Color**: Primary colors + black + white, flat fills
- **Spacing**: Mathematical precision, grid-visible
- **Details**: Circles, triangles, squares as decorative elements. Diagonal lines.
- **Shadows**: None — flat shapes

### Retro-Futuristic
- **Feel**: 1970s space-age optimism
- **Typography**: Wide display (Epilogue, Red Hat Display) + mono accents
- **Color**: Orange (#F97316), cream (#FFF7ED), brown (#78350F), teal accents
- **Spacing**: Generous, rounded containers
- **Details**: Rounded corners (16px+), retro gradients, starburst shapes
- **Shadows**: Warm, medium blur

## Specialized

### Lo-Fi Zine
- **Feel**: Photocopied, handmade, DIY
- **Typography**: Mix of weights chaotically, handwriting fonts for accents
- **Color**: Black + white + one spot color (photocopier aesthetic)
- **Spacing**: Intentionally imperfect — slight rotations, uneven margins
- **Details**: Rough textures, paper grain, sticker/stamp elements, cut-out shapes
- **Shadows**: None — flat/collage

### Glass Morphism
- **Feel**: Translucent, layered, futuristic
- **Typography**: Light weight sans (Satoshi light, Outfit light) — airy
- **Color**: Translucent whites (`rgba(255,255,255,0.1)`), vibrant gradient backgrounds
- **Spacing**: Generous padding inside glass elements
- **Details**: `backdrop-filter: blur(20px)`, subtle borders (`1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.2)`), gradient backgrounds behind glass
- **Shadows**: Colored ambient shadows from background showing through

### Data Dashboard
- **Feel**: Information-dense, command center
- **Typography**: Tabular numerals (JetBrains Mono for data), sans for labels (Satoshi)
- **Color**: Dark surface, semantic color coding (green=up, red=down), muted grays for structure
- **Spacing**: Dense but organized — clear hierarchy between panels
- **Details**: Subtle grid lines, sparklines, status indicators, monospace numbers
- **Shadows**: Minimal — inset borders for panel definition

### Luxury Product
- **Feel**: High-end, aspirational, tactile
- **Typography**: Thin serif display (Cormorant, Bodoni Moda thin) + refined sans body
- **Color**: Rich darks, gold/champagne metallics, cream
- **Spacing**: Extremely generous — space IS the luxury
- **Details**: Subtle textures, fine lines, elegant transitions, restrained motion
- **Shadows**: Subtle, multi-layered for depth

### Playful/Toy
- **Feel**: Friendly, approachable, delightful
- **Typography**: Rounded (Quicksand, Wotfard), bouncy weights
- **Color**: Candy pastels, saturated primaries, playful combinations
- **Spacing**: Generous padding, rounded containers
- **Details**: Bouncy animations, emoji accents, blob shapes, illustrations
- **Shadows**: Colorful, soft, friendly

### Industrial/Utilitarian
- **Feel**: Function-first, no-nonsense
- **Typography**: Condensed grotesque or mono — efficient, space-saving
- **Color**: Zinc/slate grays, single warning-color accent (red or amber)
- **Spacing**: Tight, efficient — maximize information density
- **Details**: Visible structure, exposed grid, label-heavy, status-focused
- **Shadows**: None — flat, bordered panels
